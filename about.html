A Brief History of the Keeks

Kiku was born on Mother's Day, 2011, in a bathroom. We didn't think her mother could have kittens, so Kiku and her sister were surprises, to say the least. We rehomed her sister, but I was able to keep Kiku, and we've been besties ever since. She used to be very skittish, but with a lot of TLC, she has come out of her shell and will gladly accept attention from anyone who wishes to bestow it upon her.

What is Kiku Up To Now?

Anything she darn well pleases. She is an old lady, but still enjoys chasing ribbons, scratching her cardboard scratchers, and making a nuisance of herself whenever someone doesn't share their tuna. Her hobbies also include napping, yelling, and supervising our resident IT man.
